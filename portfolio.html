Natural Interaction Framework

Client - IBM Research

A framework that allows a developer to facilitate conversational features of an app through enablement of natural understanding of domain-specific commands by the user and support for intelligent follow-up questions and responses.


The goal is to Empower any developer to speech enable their app in a few steps without having them to deal with speech recognizers, natural understanding models etc. so they could focus on the functionality of their app.

Skill Gap Analysis

Client - IBM Research

Most people know a specific skill that they wish to acquire.But they do not know how to acquire them, i.e. which courses to take, and at what proficiency.Not knowing the specific gaps that need to be filled, results in inefficient learning by the learner.So the ideation for Developing a system to map the current proficiencies of a user on a skill dependency graph that are required to be proficient in a given skill .The learning plan is different for each individual.The amount of proficiency required for a given skill (i.e. pre-requisites) may also differ for each user, based on what they already know

EyeSpyVR

Paper Accepted at - UBICOMP 2018

We describe our software-only eye sensing implementation — which we call EyeSpyVR — and present the results of a series of studies involving 70 participants. We demonstrate useful eye-sensing capabilities without requiring additional or specialized hardware, allowing advanced features to be enabled with little more than a software update. We envision VR application developers leveraging these techniques to open new interactive possibilities and more immersive VR experiences on commodity VR headsets.
